What is PM-KISAN?

üéØ Objective

PM-KISAN is a central sector scheme launched on 24th February 2019 to provide direct income support to all landholding farmers' families across the country to supplement their financial needs for procuring various inputs related to agriculture and allied activities.

üí∞ Financial Support

Under this scheme, an amount of ‚Çπ6,000 per year is transferred directly to the bank accounts of eligible farmers in three equal installments of ‚Çπ2,000 each, every four months.

üèõÔ∏è Implementation

The scheme is implemented by the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India, with complete funding from the Central Government.

üìä Coverage

The scheme covers all landholding farmers regardless of the size of their land holdings, making it one of the largest direct benefit transfer programs in the world.

Eligibility Criteria

‚úÖ Who Can Apply

  • All landholding farmers (including sharecroppers)
  • Farmers with cultivable land
  • Rural and urban farmers
  • Individual farmers and farmer families
  • Tenant farmers (in some states)

‚ùå Who Cannot Apply

  • Institutional landholders
  • Farmer families with any member as:
  • - Current/former MPs, MLAs, MLCs
  • - Current/former Mayors, Chairpersons
  • - Current/former Ministers
  • - Constitutional post holders
  • - Regular employees of Central/State Government
  • - Pensioners with monthly pension >‚Çπ10,000
  • - Income tax payers
  • - Professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, CA, architects)

üìã Additional Criteria

  • Must have valid Aadhaar number
  • Bank account should be linked to Aadhaar
  • Land records should be updated
  • Must be an Indian citizen
  • Age should be 18 years or above

How to Apply

1

Visit Official Website

Go to the official PM-KISAN portal at pmkisan.gov.in and click on "Farmers Corner" section.

2

New Farmer Registration

Click on "New Farmer Registration" and select your state and district. Choose between Rural/Urban farmer type.

3

Enter Aadhaar Details

Provide your Aadhaar number, captcha code, and click "Search". Your details will be auto-populated if available.

4

Fill Application Form

Complete the registration form with personal details, bank account information, and land details.

5

Submit Application

Review all information carefully and submit the application. You will receive a registration number for future reference.

6

Verification & Approval

Your application will be verified by local authorities. Once approved, you will start receiving benefits.
